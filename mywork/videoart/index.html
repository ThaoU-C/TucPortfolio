Inner Tranquility, single screen video

Type

Personal, class project

Tools

Premiere Pro, Audacity

Timeline

2 weeks; May 8 - 22, 2022


The video shows the beauty and serenity of common activities and objects. The scenes and audio aim to help the viewer feel calm and relax to connect with their inner soul. Through this project, I continue to explore the extraordinary side of familiar and ordinary objects and activities. I investigate things through different perspectives, using closeups or panoramas, to find their exquisiteness.
