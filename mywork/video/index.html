Portfolio About Resume


Inner Tranquility, single screen video


Type

Personal, class project

Tools

Premiere Pro, Audacity

Timeline

2 weeks; May 8 - 22, 2022



The video shows the beauty and serenity of common activities and objects. The scenes and audios aim to help the audience feel calm and relax to connect with their inner soul. Through this project, I continue to explore another side of familiar objects and activities, which I interact with every day when going out. I investigate things through different perspectives, using closeups or panoramas to find their exquisiteness.
