Giant Yellow Head, Paper Collage, 2020

I love working with patterns, colors, and shapes. This artwork is created to demonstrate the interaction of vivid colors, unique shapes, and strange patterns in a surreal composition. The original artwork is a paper cutout and composed into a digital collage. The Audacity is used to glitch the collage to create chaotic ​distortion and decomposition of the paper cutouts' original patterns and forms.
