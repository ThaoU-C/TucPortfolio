Sacred Forest, 3D Environment

Type

Personal, class project

Tools

Blender, Maya, Unity

Timeline

2 months, March - May, 2023



Sacred Forest depicts a cemetery environment at night. The player will wander around the cemetery as a kid with a lamp in hand looking for a playground. The lamp will illuminate the environment when the player moves, so the player can find a gate to get out of the cemetery and get to the playground. The game hopes to bring a spooky feeling to players as they wander in the darkness.

The idea for this was based on my experience as a child. I once went to a cemetery to play with childhood friends at night and experienced peculiar events. After the trip, I dreamt about getting lost in a cemetery for a whole month. For every night that month, I saw myself being stuck in the dark cemetery, running and screaming, but unable to find the playground or get out of the place.

Sacred Forest was built in Unity and can be explored HERE
