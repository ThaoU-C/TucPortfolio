Graphic design for Tiki Corporation

I was a graphic designer at Tiki and collaborated with the Marketing and Product teams. My role was to produce compelling visual designs and landing pages with high quality product images in a timely manner and to ensure consistent design quality across multiple platforms. Agility in creating new, effective, and efficient designs was a required competency to reflect the ever changing needs of customers in an e-commerce environment.

Finalized designs were resized to various sizes to fit across multiple social media and advertising platforms.
